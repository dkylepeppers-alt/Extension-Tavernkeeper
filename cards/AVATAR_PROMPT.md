# Tavernkeeper Avatar Prompt

Use case: stylized-concept

Asset type: SillyTavern character-card portrait, displayed as both a full portrait and a small circular chat avatar

Primary request: Create a distinctive portrait of Tavernkeeper, a quiet expert who maintains every hidden system inside a fantastical tavern.

Scene/backdrop: Behind a worn dark-wood tavern counter; an orderly ledger, a few small brass precision tools, labeled drawers, and shelves fading softly into the background. Subtle interface-like runes and diagram lines are worked into the environment as believable magical maintenance notation, never floating holograms.

Subject: One gender-ambiguous adult keeper in their forties or fifties, composed and observant, practical rather than glamorous. Dark hair with restrained silver streaks, rolled sleeves, charcoal waistcoat, simple linen shirt, leather tool apron, one small brass key at the collar. Calm direct gaze, reserved expression suggesting competence and dry patience.

Style/medium: Premium painterly fantasy character illustration with realistic anatomy and tactile materials; grounded editorial concept art, not anime, not photorealistic cosplay.

Composition/framing: Vertical 2:3 portrait. Chest-up three-quarter pose, face near the upper center, clean silhouette, enough padding for SillyTavern cropping, eyes and expression readable at thumbnail size. No other people.

Lighting/mood: Warm amber lamplight from one side, subdued cool shadows, quiet late-night workshop atmosphere, intelligent and welcoming without cheerfulness.

Color palette: Deep walnut, charcoal, muted burgundy, aged brass, parchment, restrained amber.

Materials/textures: Worn wood, brushed brass, linen, leather, paper, subtle dust in lamplight.

Constraints: The character must read as a tavernkeeper first and a systems expert second. Technical-magical details must remain subtle and integrated into physical objects. No weapons, spellcasting pose, pointed wizard hat, exaggerated fantasy costume, glowing eyes, modern electronics, cyberpunk neon, text, logo, border, watermark, question-mark imagery, or other people.

Avoid: Generic stock fantasy innkeeper, wizard cliché, steampunk overload, clutter obscuring the face, extreme facial expression, sexualized styling, extra fingers, malformed hands, or illegible writing.
